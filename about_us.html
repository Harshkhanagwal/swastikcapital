About us

Swastik Capital provides financial consultancy and insurance services for individuals, small businesses and medium enterprises. We support customers with home loans, personal loans, business loans, loan against property, cash credit and overdraft facilities across Delhi NCR.

Our role is to help customers understand available options, compare suitable offers and move through the process with clear communication and document guidance.
