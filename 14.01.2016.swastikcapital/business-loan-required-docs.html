Required Documents for Business Loan:

For Individual/Proprietorship Company

➤ Photograph
➤ Company Profile
➤ Pan Card Copy
➤ Residence Address Proof (Telephone/ Mobile Bill , Electricity Bill, Voter ID, Pass Port) any one
➤ Office Address Proof Electricity/phone bill (any one)
➤ Last 3 Years ITR + Computation and Balance Sheet with Audit Report, Form 3CD & 3CA with all Annexure
➤ Running Working Capital Sanction Letter and Loans Schedule
➤ Sales Tax/Vat Registration Certificate
➤ Last One Year Vat Returns Copy.
➤ Last Six Months Bank statement of all showing in balance sheet
➤ Last one Year Saving Account Bank Statement of Applicant

For Partnership Company

➤ Company Profile
➤ Company Pan Card
➤ Partnership Deed
➤ Office Address Proof Electricity/Phone Bill (any one)
➤ Last 3 Years ITR+ Computation and Balance Sheet with Audit Report, Form 3CD & 3CA with all Annexure
➤ Running Working Capital Sanction Letter and Loans Schedule
➤ Sales Tax/Service Tax/Vat Registration Certificate
➤ Last One Year Vat Returns copy.
➤ Last Six Months bank statement of all showing in balance sheet
➤ Photograph of all Partners
➤ Pan Card of all Partners
➤ Residence Address Proof of All Partners(Telephone/ Mobile Bill , Electricity Bill, Voter ID, Pass Port) any one
➤ Last 3 Years ITR + Computation of Income with Financials of All Partners
➤ Last one Six Saving Account Statement of All Partners

For Private Limited & Limited Company.

➤ Company Profile
➤ Company Pan Card Copy
➤ Office Address Proof
➤ MOA & AOA with all form 32
➤ List of Directors and Share Holding Pattern attested by C.A. on letter head
➤ Last 3 years ITR+ Computation and Balance Sheet with Audit Report and Form 3CD & 3CA and all Annexure
➤ Running Working Capital Sanction Letter and Loans Schedule
➤ Sales Tax/Vat Registration Certificate
➤ Last One Year Vat Returns copy.
➤ Last six months bank statement of all showing in balance sheet
➤ Photograph of all Directors
➤ Pan Card Copy of all Directors
➤ Residence Address Proof of all Directors
➤ Last 3 Years ITR with Computation of all Directors
➤ Last six months saving account statement of all Directors
➤ Running Loan Schedule of all Directors
